About Zahari Creatives

Zahari Creatives empowers children and youth through structured creative learning — combining music education, media skills, and beginner STEM to build well-rounded learners. We partner with schools and organisations to run after-school clubs, workshops, and in-school programmes that are practical, curriculum-aligned and hugely engaging.

  • Experienced instructors
    Practitioners with teaching & industry experience.
  • Practical outcomes
    Performances, portfolios & certificates.
  • School-friendly
    Flexible delivery: clubs, term courses, workshops.
  • Safe & inclusive
    Child-centered safeguarding and pastoral care.
